Sushi Bake Recipe

Description

Sushi bake is the perfect dish to bring with you to parties! It's delicious, easy to make, and the ingredients readily available at your local supermarket and Asian grocer.

This guaranteed crowd favorite can be prepared within an hour, making it the ideal choice for those tight on time and busybodies. You will only need the following ingredients...

Ingredients:

Sushi Rice

  • 2 cups of uncooked sushi Rice
  • 2 1/4 cups of water
  • Sesame oil to taste
  • Furikake rice seasoning

Salmon & Crab Layer

  • 1 lb salmon
  • 1 lb imitation crab meat
  • 4 oz cream cheese
  • Kewpie mayo
  • Sriracha
  • Green onions

Topping

  • Kewpie mayo
  • Sriracha
  • Furikake rice seasoning

Instructions

Sushi Rice

  1. Rinse rice to rid of the starch, then add to rice cooker with water and set to cook.
  2. Once rice is cooked, place rice in mixing bowl and add a teaspoon of sesame oil to taste, gently fold.
  3. Sprinkle in some furikake rice seasoning, and gently fold the mixture again.
  4. Deposite rice into an oven-safe casserole dish and spread evenly. Sprinkle another layer of furikake atop the rice and set aside.

Salmon & Crab Layer

  1. Preheat oven to 400F.
  2. Place salmon on greased baking pan and place in oven once oven is done preheating (temperature should be at 400F). Let it bake for 15 minutes.
