Canned Tuna Rice Bowl

Description

Too lazy to fillet and cook a whole tuna? Our tuna rice bowl has you covered!

This recipe is quick and easy. There's not a lot of cooking - just the rice. But if you already have a bowl of day old leftover rice from last night (or last last night's) dinner, then you're good to go! All you need for this recipe is...

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup of rice
  • Canned tuna
  • 2 teaspoons of soy sauce
  • Kewpie mayo
  • Sriracha
  • Nori seaweed

Instructions

(This recipe assumes that you already have cooked rice on hand. If not, then please cook at least 1 cup of white rice.)

  1. Put one serving of rice in a bowl and set aside.
  2. Open canned tuna and strain out the oils/water from the meat, then place the fish atop the bed of rice, gently mix.
  3. Put 2 teaspoons of soy sauce in the rice, as well as a drizzle of Kewpie mayou and sriracha to your desired amount. Mix thorughly.

Enjoy your delicious canned tuna rice bowl with nori seawood for added flavor!
